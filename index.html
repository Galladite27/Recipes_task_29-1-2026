Laurie's toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1/2oz butter

Instructions:

  1. Put bread in toaster
  2. Turn on toaster
  3. Take bread out of toaster
  4. Butter toast

O'Shea's cake

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. 1. Combine milk, eggs and sugar
  2. 2. Add flour to the wet mix
  3. 3. Preheat oven
  4. 4. Place cake mix into a baking tray
