ZinZin's Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 180 grams of unsalted butter
  • 180 grams of dark chocolate chips
  • 40 grams of cocoa powder
  • 80 grams all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275 grams of sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt

Instructions:

  1. Let the unsalted butter sit in room temperature
  2. Mix the butter with the dark chocolate chips in a bowl until combined. You can also do a bouble boil with saucepan with water or even microwave it for 30 seconds
  3. In another larger bowl, add all the dry ingredients together in small batches and stir them well together
  4. Add eggs into the dry mixture and stir well
  5. Follow by adding the mixture of the melted chocolate and butter mixture into the mixture bowl
  6. Stir well until fully combined
  7. Pour batter into the baking tray or your choosing of container
  8. Bake your mixture for 180 degrees for 40 minutes
